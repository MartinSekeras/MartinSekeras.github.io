A Little About Me

“Code is like humor. When you have to explain it, it’s bad.” – Cory House

This is not be all and end all as I've had some experience with other languages like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Action Script, Java, Visual Basic and I have National Certificate in Computer Systems Development.
